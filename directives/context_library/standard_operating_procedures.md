# Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) - Content Drafting

1. **Research Phase**: Identify primary and secondary keywords. Gather at least 3 high-authority sources.
2. **Outline Phase**: Create a H1, H2, and H3 hierarchy. Ensure logical flow.
3. **Drafting Phase**: Incorporate "Fake RAG" context from `context_library/`.
4. **QA Phase**: Check for SEO optimization, readability (Flesch-Kincaid), and brand voice alignment.
